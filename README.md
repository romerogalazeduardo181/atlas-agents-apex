# Atlas Agents Apex

Atlas Agents Apex builds specialized digital employees powered by Apex Core.

## Vision

Atlas Agents Apex is an artificial intelligence company focused on building specialized digital employees for businesses.

The first digital employee is Apex SDR, designed to help companies qualify leads, follow up with prospects, and book commercial meetings.

## Core Concept

Apex Core is the operating system behind all Apex Digital Employees.

Initial Apex Core modules:

* Brain: decides what the agent should do.
* Knowledge: stores company information, documents, FAQs, and context.
* Conversations: manages messages, history, channels, and dialogue state.
* Actions: executes tasks such as sending messages, creating tasks, or booking meetings.
* Dashboard: displays results, metrics, and agent performance.

## First Product

Apex SDR is the first commercial product of Atlas Agents Apex.

Its mission is to convert leads into qualified commercial meetings.

## July 2026 Goal

The goal for July 2026 is to get the first paying client by selling Apex SDR as an AI implementation service, while gradually building reusable Apex Core components.
