# Apex SDR — Commercial Offer v1.0

## Product-Service

Apex SDR is a specialized digital employee designed to help companies convert leads into qualified commercial meetings.

## Value Proposition

Your first digital employee specialized in getting high-quality commercial meetings, available forever.

## Core Promise

Apex SDR helps your business respond faster, qualify prospects, follow up consistently, and book more commercial meetings.

## Ideal Customer

Apex SDR is designed for companies that:

* Receive leads through Instagram, WhatsApp, website forms, email, or ads.
* Sell services with medium or high ticket value.
* Need to respond faster to potential clients.
* Want more qualified meetings for their sales process.

## Initial Target Segment

Small and medium marketing agencies.

## Main Problem

Many agencies generate attention, traffic, and leads, but lose opportunities because:

* They respond late.
* They forget to follow up.
* They qualify prospects manually.
* They depend too much on the founder or sales team.
* They lack a consistent sales conversation process.

## Apex SDR Solution

Apex SDR helps by:

* Receiving new leads.
* Starting the first conversation.
* Asking qualification questions.
* Identifying commercial intent.
* Suggesting or sending follow-up messages.
* Preparing qualified prospects for a sales meeting.
* Tracking lead status and commercial opportunities.

## Initial Delivery

The first version is delivered as a hybrid implementation:

* Manual setup.
* AI-assisted conversation flows.
* Lead qualification structure.
* Follow-up templates.
* Simple dashboard or tracking system.
* Weekly performance review.
