# Apex SDR — Demo v1.0

## Demo Objective

Show how Apex SDR helps a company respond faster, qualify leads, follow up consistently, and book more commercial meetings.

## Demo Duration

2 minutes.

## Demo Structure

### 1. Business Context

A company receives leads through Instagram, WhatsApp, website forms, email, or ads.

Many of those leads need fast response, qualification, and follow-up before they become sales opportunities.

Apex SDR acts as a digital sales employee that manages this first commercial layer.

---

## 2. Example Scenario

A marketing agency receives a new lead through Instagram.

### Lead Message

“Hi, I’m interested in your marketing services. How much do you charge?”

### Apex SDR Response

“Hi, thanks for reaching out. I can help you find the best option. What type of business do you have?”

### Lead Response

“I have a dental clinic.”

### Apex SDR Qualification

“Perfect. Are you looking to get more patient bookings, improve your social media presence, or run paid ads?”

### Lead Response

“We want more patient bookings.”

### Apex SDR Follow-Up

“Great. That usually works well with a lead generation campaign. Are you currently investing in ads or would this be your first campaign?”

### Lead Response

“We have tried ads before, but results were inconsistent.”

### Apex SDR Meeting Suggestion

“Understood. The best next step is a short strategy call so the team can review your current situation and suggest a clear plan. Would you like to schedule a 15-minute call this week?”

---

## 3. Follow-Up Example

If the lead stops responding, Apex SDR can follow up:

“Hi, I wanted to follow up on your interest in getting more patient bookings. If you want, we can schedule a short call and review the best approach for your clinic.”

---

## 4. What the Client Sees

The client sees:

* New leads received.
* Lead status.
* Qualification answers.
* Suggested next step.
* Follow-up status.
* Meetings booked.

---

## 5. Demo Explanation Script

“Here is how Apex SDR works.

When a new lead arrives, the digital employee responds quickly, understands the prospect’s need, asks qualification questions, and guides the conversation toward a commercial meeting.

The goal is to help the business capture more opportunities from the leads it already receives.

For the first version, we implement Apex SDR as a hybrid system: part AI, part structured sales process, part tracking dashboard.

The result is a more consistent sales process and better follow-up.”
