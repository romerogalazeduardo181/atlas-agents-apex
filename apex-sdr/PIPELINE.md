## Sales Conversation Goal

The goal of the first conversation is not to explain all the technology.

The goal is to discover:

* How many leads they receive.
* How fast they respond.
* How they follow up.
* How they qualify prospects.
* How many meetings they book.
* Whether they see value in Apex SDR.

---

## Key Questions for Discovery

Ask:

1. How do new leads usually contact your agency?
2. How many leads do you receive per month?
3. Who responds to those leads?
4. How fast do you usually respond?
5. Do you have a follow-up process?
6. What happens when a lead asks for prices?
7. How do you decide if a lead is qualified?
8. How many sales meetings do you usually book per month?
9. What would improve your sales process the most?
10. If Apex SDR helped you book more qualified meetings, would that be valuable for your agency?
